§ 0 Research Hypotheses
H₁ — Menu Selection
Chicken is the highest-frequency protein selection by unit count (n). Steak and salmon are greater than or equal to chicken in gross revenue contribution despite lower unit frequency. Rare proteins (ahi tuna, sea bass, shrimp) aggregate to a statistically non-random distribution when tested against day of week (DOTW).
H₂ — Shift × DOTW Interaction
Day shift and night shift sales distributions are not independent of day of week. χ² test of independence applied across the full 30-day window.
H₀ — Null
All menu selections follow a uniform random distribution. Shift totals are independent of DOTW. No significant correlation exists between protein category and daily gross.
