§ 01

Framework Introduction

This document is the working scaffold for the K(t) research project — formally titled The Jonah Study. The central argument: what appears as individual choice, behavioral pattern, or personal trajectory is in fact a measurable structural output. The constraint is not metaphor. It is function. K(t) is the formal name for that function.


The research spans six analytical layers, a mathematical proof anchored to a 39% structural constant, and a convergence result that holds across two centuries of independent data. The provisional patent filing (March 27, 2026) establishes priority on both the formula and the framework architecture.
